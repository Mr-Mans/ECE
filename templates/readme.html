How it works!

Coleman-Liau Index

  • The Coleman-Liau Index is a readability test designed to show the reading level of a text. It uses sentences and letters as variables.
    It is calculated using the formula:
    (0.0588 * L) - (0.296 * S) - 15.8
    where:
    L is the average number of letters per 100 words.
    S is the average number of sentences per 100 words.


Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level

  • The Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level is a readability test which assesses the approximate reading grade level of a text. It takes into account the length of the sentence, number of sylables as well as the word length.
    It is calculated based on the formula:
    0.39 * (total words / total sentences) + 11.8 * (total syllables / total words) - 15.59.


Readability Score

  • The Readability Score is obtained by obtaining the average of both the Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level score and the Coleman-Liau Index score
    Coleman-Liau Index score + Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level / 2 .


Lexical Analysis

  • Lexical analysis examines the vocabulary of the essay. It includes the calculation of word count, average word length, unique words count, vocabulary size, and Type-Token Ratio (TTR).

  • Type-Token Ratio (TTR) - Type-Token Ratio (TTR) is a measure of vocabulary richness that assesses the diversity of words in a text. It is calculated by dividing the number of unique words (types) by the total number of words (tokens) in a given segment of language.
    A higher TTR suggests greater lexical variety in the text, indicating a richer and more diverse vocabulary. The closer the TTR ratio is to 1, the greater the lexical richness of the segment.
    TTR=( Total Number of Words / Number of Unique Words)


Sentence Structure Analysis

  • Sentence structure analysis categorizes sentences into short, medium, and long based on their length;number of words in each sentence.

    • Short Sentences: - Sentences containing 8 words or fewer.
    • Medium Sentences: - Sentences containing more than 8 words but 16 words or fewer.
    • Long Sentences - Sentences containing more than 16 words.
